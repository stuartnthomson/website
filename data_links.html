Links to Online Data Repositories
Last Updated: Feb 28th, 2024
I have had a number of requests asking for the full apatite fission track count data, full apatite fission track length measurements, and more complete tables of (U-Th)/He data from some of my studies for the purposes of conducting inverse thermal history modeling using HeFTy, QTQt, and/or Pecube, or other quantitataive analyses.

Below are links to a few data sets that I have recently uploaded to online data repositories that now include complete data (e.g. full single grains apatite fission track count data with Dpar values, or full sample apatite fission track length data with angle to c-axis and Dpar values) that were not included in the tables or supplemental data of the original publication(s).

If you need full fission track count data, or any other data, from my other publications, please don't hesistate to contact me
